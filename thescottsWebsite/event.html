THE EVENT

Home / THE EVENT

Sailing with the Scotts 2018 is your chance to go on vacation with Jonathan and Drew! Enjoy exotic ports of call and amazing celebrity-hosted interactive events, including panel discussions and workshops, game show nights, dance parties and karaoke contests! Don’t miss the brothers’ engaging presentations, including a behind-the-scenes look at the latest season of Brothers vs Brother, and a revealing session of 20 Questions, submitted by you and your fellow cruisers. The brothers will also host shore excursions, meet and greets, photo sessions, and dance lessons! Don’t miss the shoppable Scott Living Showroom at Sea for a sneak peek at new pieces from the brothers’ home goods collection.

You'll enjoy five days and four nights aboard Royal Caribbean's newly renovated Mariner of the Seas®, where you can savor the delicacies of delicious restaurants like celebrity chef Jamie Oliver’s Jamie’s Italian, fresh-rolled sushi at Izumi, and steak and lobster at Chop House. Get your morning caffeine fix at the onboard Starbucks, jitterbug over to Johnny Rockets for burgers and fries in the afternoon, and then swing into The Bamboo Room tiki lounge for a kitschy-cool throwback cocktail.

Looking for adventure?

Try something new! Choose from oodles of onboard activities including mystery dinner theater, foreign language classes, wine tastings, jewelry-making workshops, scrapbooking sessions, ice skating, and Escape Room challenges. Gather your friends for a game of laser tag, or check out the all-new Sky Pad™ bungee trampoline experience. On the upper deck, you can relax at the poolside bar, watch movies on a poolside screen, learn how to surf on a 40-foot-long surf simulator, or race down The Perfect Storm™ high-speed waterslide. Don’t miss this spectacular opportunity to escape to the Bahamas while learning valuable design tips & tricks with the Scott brothers. Come sail away with us!
