Talk about smooth sailing. The action-packed Mariner of the Seas® offers brand-new ways to play, including the Sky Pad℠ virtual reality bungee trampoline, the FlowRider® surf simulator, and The Perfect Storm℠ waterslides. These thrilling experiences join can’t-miss ship favorites like laser tag, original stage productions, and a rock-climbing wall with stunning views from the top. Once you’ve worked up an appetite, grab Tuscan eats at Jamie’s Italian by Jamie Oliver, indulge in fresh-rolled sushi at Izumi, or have a cozy date night at the tiki-themed Bamboo Room. Finally, retreat to the comfort of your stateroom for deluxe amenities like 24-hour room service and the fastest Internet at sea.
