There’s never a dull moment when Sailing with the Scotts. Drew and Jonathan have planned a boatload of exciting experiences, including:

  • Celebrity-hosted interactive events, like panel discussions and workshops
  • Game show nights
  • Themed dance parties and karaoke contests
  • A behind-the-scenes look at the latest season of Brothers vs. Brother
  • A revealing session of 20 Questions—they’ll answer your burning questions!
  • Hosted shore excursions
  • Meet and greets and other photo ops
  • Dance lessons
  • A shoppable Scott Living Showroom at Sea, where you can get a sneak peek at newpieces from Drew and Jonathan’s home collection

Plus, don’t miss out on the Mariner of the Seas®’ onboard offerings, like the Sky Pad℠ virtual reality bungee trampoline, the FlowRider® surf simulator, The Perfect Storm℠ waterslides, glow-in-the-dark laser tag, original stage productions, an epic rock-climbing wall, Playmakers Sports Bar & Arcade, Escape Room: The Observatorium, and much more.
